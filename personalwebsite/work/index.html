Prospect Research Analyst, Drake University

Currently, I'm putting my research skills to use helping the University Advancement team connect with alumni. The majority of my time is spent analyzing and sythesizing information to assess a prospect's financial capacity, philanthropic tendencies, wealth capacity and affinity to Drake University. After being in my role for a year, the project I'm most proud of is creating an Alumni Engagement Score for Drake's alumni.

Sudent Assistant, Robinson Map Library, UW-Madison

While in graduate school, I worked at the Robinson Map Library creating metadata for local geospacial data collections. I also provided reference services to students and community members. One of my favorite parts of this job was helping Wisconsin farmers find arial photos of their land from the 1940s to the present.

Prospect Research Student Associate, Wisconsin Foundation and Alumni Association

This position expanded my notion of what I could do post library school. I realized that the many aspects of library science I love could be found outside of the library. Helping people find information they are looking for is what brings me joy, no matter the space.

AVID Tutor, Madison Metropolitan School District
